Nymeria Da Bear

"Greatest Puppy Ever!" -self-quote

Nymeria Da Bear is a German Shepherd dog born in Ontario, Canada. She just recently turned 4 years old on March 30th, 2018. She enjoys playing off-leash with other dogs or in a park with her soccer ball and frisbees. She has taken obedience classes and scent detection courses. She is currently on a diet to lose some winter weigth.
