Nymeria Da Bear

"Greatest Puppy Ever!" -self-quote

She enjoys playing off-leash with other dogs or in a park with her soccer ball and frisbees. She has taken obedience classes and scent detection courses. She is currently on a diet to lose some winter weigth.
